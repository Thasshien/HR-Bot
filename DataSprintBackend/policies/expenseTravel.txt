Expense & Travel Reimbursement

- Food Allowance (on trips): ₹800/day.
- Hotel Stay: Up to ₹3,000/night (Metro Cities), ₹2,000/night (Non-Metro).
- Travel: Economy airfare if > 500 km, Train AC 2-tier if < 500 km.
- Cab fare within trip location is reimbursable.
- Bills must be submitted within 30 days with original receipts.

Non-Reimbursable:
- Alcohol
- Personal shopping
- Entertainment expenses
